Do It Yourself

Do It Yourself was a pre-graVITas Workshop conducted by IEEE- PCS on 16th September. The director of Indian telecom services Mr P. Murali lead the 6 hour long seminar followed by a hands-on-session. The workshop saw enthusiastic participation of over 60+ students. The participants were provided with take-away kits and damaged phones which they repaired under constant guidance of experts.

Place It

In the season of job offers and placements, we present to you a Placement based seminar "Place It!". Get an opportunity to interact with the recently placed students in dream and super-dream companies. Get an insight of their experience and tips and tricks to crack the interviews! All the 3rd and 4th year students from all branches are welcome to join the league!!!

Takeshi's Castle

With more than 100 participants, fighting from water-guns to water balloons , 3 Day Long event – Takeshi’s Castle gave us all some mesmerizing memories and unlimited fun. The first two days witnessed immense number of participants but the last day saw a turn around with the highest ratio of participants in the three day long run process. Students as well as externals enjoyed the fun event to the fullest and created some beautiful moments at the Kalpana Chawla Ground.

1300 Reasons Why

Amidst the excitement of one of the biggest Technical Fests of the country, we at IEEE-PCS helped many create memories of playing a very distinct game of 1300 reasons why. It was conducted on 22nd and 23rd of September and gave the students of VIT along with the external participants an opportunity to prove that they are in fact, a master of all when their abilities were put to test. From finding the optimal solution to a coding problem to scoring the highest in Road Rash along with putting two and two together to complete the Treasure Hunt inside TT, we made sure that the participants have an experience they'll remember. We stand proud on making the event a memorable one.
