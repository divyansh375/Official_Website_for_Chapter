Story

IEEE Professional Communication Society was established in the year 2011 under the esteemed guidance of faculty coordinator, Dr. Anitha Devi. V. It is recognized as a dynamic body of enthusiastic and technically spirited engineers with a desire to create a change with innovation. PCS (Professional Communication Society) is a brother society of IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) aimed at improving one’s communication skills in engineering, scientific and other technical environments. The major objective of IEEE PCS is to help engineers, technically to communicate better in their work place both verbally and non-verbally and also promotes and facilitates leading edge education and training in professional environments. PCS has conducted a variety of events over the past few years like Cell Tech (Do it yourself workshop), Polyglot, Magnus, Bazinga Reloaded and Scholasticus power which have attracted students from all branches to showcase their communication, technical and management skills.

Mission

To foster a community which will be dedicated towards the understanding of engineering, scientific, technical environments et al. At the same time, remaining in pursuit of promoting effective communication in all of these dimensions. The outreach activities of the professional communication society include publishing quotes, running vocabulary building series, posting know our team series in addition to some occasional, witty idioms and proverbs. The weekly editorial insights are what singularly define the chapter's objective whereas, the chapter's blog gives the much-needed platform for its members to express and share their views and opinions. A much clearer perspective on our chapter's functioning can be taken from the chapter's website.
